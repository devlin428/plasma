Setup:
	Each player in turn places all pieces
	Computer first
	Coin flip in VS determines order

Turn Order:
	Human over computer
	The order in setup

Turn:
	* Selects 1 unit
		* Move unit across the board
		* Attack

Upkeep:
	Each unit gain energy by Max / (Current Units - 2)

Unit Stats:
	HP
	Attack
	Defence
	Energy
	Movement limit
	Attack range
	Movement Energy Cost
	Attack Energy Cost

Battle calculation:
	Attack - Defense = Damage to HP

Goal:
	Reach a square
